TIPS FOR KEEPING YOUR PETS HEALTHY DURING WINTER.

  1. TIP:Bring your pets inside during cold weather.
  2. TIP:Protect their paws.
  3. TIP:Take care of their coat and skin.
  4. TIP:Antifreeze is a deadly poison.
  5. TIP:Use space heaters with caution.
  6. TIP:Know your pet's limits outdoors.
